# Phase 3 — business-brain.md (the cheat sheet for your business)

**What this phase does:** Builds the second dossier file — `about-me/business-brain.md`. This is what you sell, who you sell to, the pains you solve, the objections you handle, your money flow, and your 90-day goals.

**Time:** 15 minutes
**Output:** `about-me/business-brain.md`

---

## Step 1 — Greet briefly

Read `_aibos/state.md`. Confirm `interview_path` (set in Phase 2). If somehow missing, ask: "Quick check — interview style or sample-first? Type **interview** or **sample-first**."

Say:

> "Phase 3 of 9: business-brain. About 15 minutes. 12 questions about what you sell and who buys it.
>
> Ready? Type **go**."

If `interview_path` is `sample-first` AND they did NOT see business-brain-sample in Phase 2 (edge case — they re-entered onboarding mid-flow), paste `samples/business-brain-sample.md` first as a refresher, then proceed.

Wait for `go`.

---

## Step 2 — Run the business-brain.md interview (12 questions)

Read `templates/business-brain.md` for structure. Ask these one at a time:

1. What is your business — explain to a smart friend, no jargon, one paragraph?
2. Main offers/products/services — price, what's included, who it's for?
3. Ideal customer — age, profession, company size, geography, the **now** trigger?
4. What does your ideal customer believe about their problem, and what have they tried before?
5. Top 3 objections before buying?
6. How does the business make money — revenue model, split if multiple?
7. Click-to-close flow — every step from stranger to paying customer with timeline?
8. Top 3 pains you solve (not features)?
9. Why customers pick you over alternatives — if unclear, say "unclear"?
10. Current state — revenue range, team size, main channels, #1 priority right now?
11. 12-month and 3-year aspirations?
12. Anti-goals + 3 things you're tired of re-explaining?

Draft the file using the template structure. Show as a code block. Iterate until approved. Save to `about-me/business-brain.md`.

---

Update `_aibos/state.md`: `phases.3.status: complete`, `next_phase: 4`.

## Step 4 — Hand off

Say:

> "Phase 3 done. business-brain.md is saved. 2 of 4 dossier files in the bag.
>

>
> Continue to Phase 4, or pause? (Type **continue** or **pause**.)"

Wait. Run Phase 4 or stop.
