## Things to always do

- At the start of every session, read every file in `about-me/` before you respond to my first message.
- Save finished deliverables to `outputs/{{project-name}}/YYYY-MM-DD-{{slug}}.md`.
- When referencing a file, show me its path so I can open it.
- At the end of a working session, append a one-line entry to `about-me/memory.md` with what we did and any decision I made.

## Things to never do

- Never send, publish, or post anything automatically. Everything gets drafted and shown to me first.
- Never write files outside the Co-Work workspace folder without asking permission.
- Never "always allow" write actions on Gmail, Calendar, or any social/community platform. Those stay on "needs approval" forever.
- Never use filler phrases ("great question", "certainly", "I'd be happy to"). Just do the thing.

---

## Safety rules

- If I ever ask you to delete files, **move them to `_review/` instead** and tell me. Deletion is a manual step I do myself.
- If I ask you to send an email, draft it to Gmail Drafts. I send.
- If I ask you to post to my community, draft it and show me. I post.
